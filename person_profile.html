Brief Life History of Albert Kweku

Albert Kweku Obeng was born on 7 March 1962, in Winneba, Effutu Municipal, Central, Ghana as the son of Kofi Amoa-Daffour and Elizabeth Asabea Obeng. He died on 9 March 2004, in Accra, Greater Accra, Ghana, at the age of 42, and was buried in Accra, Greater Accra, Ghana.
